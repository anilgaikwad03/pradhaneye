Pradhan Eye Hospital was founded by Dr Uma Pradhan in 1984. From a modest three room set-up we have gradually grown to a spacious modern NABH accredited eye hospital with comprehensive eye-care facilities under one roof. The first IOL implant in Solapur was conducted by Dr Uma Pradhan. It has been our constant endeavor to keep acquiring new technologies & facilities and provide state-of-the-art services at very reasonable rates. We provide a whole range of diagnostic & therapeutic services including lasers for accurate and early diagnosis, treatment and follow-up of various ophthalmic ailments. Our eye-banking facility gives a ray of hope to patients suffering from corneal blindness. We acquired the Amaris 200e Excimer Laser in 2013 and introduced the best in refractive surgery technology.

Mission

Pradhan Eye Hospital and Lasik Laser Center is committed to provide comprehensive Eye care services to the patient, he emphasis being on personalized treatment of visual problems to improve the vision and thereby improve overall health, well being & Lifestyle of the patient. At Pradhan Eye Hospital and Lasik Laser Center our endeavor will be to provide excellent, state of art clinical care and surgical services through our experience & expertise. At Pradhan Eye Hospital and Lasik Laser Center patient will be our main focus and patients well being will be our sole interest.

Vision

We aim to be the leading centre in the region for eye care services through excellence in our results, constant up-gradation of scientific knowledge and technology and high degree of patient and staff satisfaction.
